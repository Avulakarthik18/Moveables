CarRentalPro — Car Owner Guide: Listing Cars for Rental (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

OVERVIEW:
Car owners (private vehicle owners) can register on CarRentalPro and list their cars either for:
1. Rental Only — Renters drive the car themselves.
2. Rental with Driver — The owner or a registered driver is attached to the car.

HOW TO LIST A CAR FOR RENTAL:

Step 1: Login to your account. Make sure you are registered as an owner.
Step 2: Go to "Add Car" section.
Step 3: Choose your listing type:
  - "Rental Only" — Car will be rented without a driver.
  - "With Driver" — Car comes with a registered driver.

LISTING A RENTAL-ONLY CAR:
Step 1: Go to Add Car > Add Car (Rental Only).
Step 2: Fill in all required details:
  - Car company, model, year
  - Fuel type, transmission
  - Registration number
  - Number of seats
  - Daily rental price (₹ per day)
  - Security deposit amount
  - Pickup location
  - Car photos
  - Any special notes or conditions
Step 3: Submit for Admin approval.
Step 4: Admin reviews the listing. Once approved, your car appears in search results.

LISTING A CAR WITH DRIVER:
Step 1: Go to Add Car > Add Car with Driver.
Step 2: Fill in the car details (same as above).
Step 3: Additionally provide driver details:
  - Driver's name and mobile
  - Driver's license number and type
  - Vehicle type the driver is registered for
Step 4: The fare for "With Driver" mode is ₹15/km based on route distance (not per day).
Step 5: Submit for Admin approval.

MANAGING YOUR CAR LISTINGS:
- Go to Add Car > My Car Status to see all your listed cars.
- You can see if each listing is Pending, Approved, or Rejected.
- Rejected listings will show the reason. Correct the details and resubmit.
- Approved listings show when they are booked, by whom, and for which dates.

IMPORTANT RULES FOR OWNERS:
- All cars must have valid insurance and RC (Registration Certificate).
- The admin may request photos of the car, insurance documents, and RC.
- Do not list cars with expired insurance or registration.
- Cars with active bookings cannot be removed from the platform.
- If you want to take your car off the platform, ensure no active bookings exist first.
- Owners are responsible for ensuring the car is clean, fueled, and ready for pickup.
- Customers rate their experience — high ratings improve your listing visibility.

PRICING TIPS FOR OWNERS:
- Set a competitive daily price by checking similar cars in your area.
- Use the platform's AI price predictor in the Sell section for market value reference.
- Security deposits should be proportional to the car's value — typically 10-20% of the car's market value per booking.

NOTIFICATIONS:
- Owners receive email notifications when a new booking is made on their car.
- When a booking is completed, the owner gets a summary with dates and total earned.
