CarRentalPro — Customer Support Guide (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

OVERVIEW:
CarRentalPro provides multiple support channels to help customers, owners, and drivers resolve issues quickly.

SUPPORT CHANNELS AVAILABLE:

1. LIVE CHATBOT (RoadMind AI):
- Available 24/7 on every page of the platform.
- Click the chat bubble icon (bottom right corner) to open RoadMind AI.
- Ask anything about bookings, parcels, car listings, policies, or car problems.
- RoadMind AI can also access your live booking and listing data if you are logged in.
- Best for: Instant answers, general queries, car advice, policy questions.

2. EMAIL SUPPORT:
- Go to Services > Customer Support > Email Support.
- Fill out the support form with your email, subject, and detailed message.
- The support team will respond within 24-48 hours.
- Best for: Detailed complaints, billing disputes, listing issues.

3. CALL SUPPORT:
- Go to Services > Customer Support > Call Support.
- Find the customer care number to call for direct assistance.
- Available during business hours (typically 9 AM – 7 PM, Monday to Saturday).
- Best for: Urgent issues, trip emergencies, driver-related complaints.

4. BOOKING ASSISTANCE:
- Go to Services > Customer Support > Booking Assistance.
- Get step-by-step help for bookings, cancellations, and modifications.
- Best for: Users who are confused about the booking process.

5. PAYMENT & REFUND SUPPORT:
- Go to Services > Customer Support > Payment & Refund Support.
- For issues with failed payments, incorrect charges, or pending refunds.
- Provide your booking ID and payment reference for faster resolution.
- Best for: Razorpay payment failures, refund delays, deposit queries.

6. FAQs AND HELP CENTER:
- Go to Services > Customer Support > FAQs & Help Center.
- Browse frequently asked questions organized by category.
- Categories include: Booking, Payments, Parcels, Sellers/Buyers, Drivers, General.
- Best for: Self-service answers to common questions.

CONTACT PAGE:
- Go to Services > Contact to see all contact methods in one place.
- Includes the office address, phone number, email, and WhatsApp link.

WHAT TO DO IN DIFFERENT SITUATIONS:

CAR BREAKDOWN DURING RENTAL:
1. Pull over safely to the side of the road.
2. Turn on hazard lights.
3. Call the owner (their number is in your booking email).
4. If no response, call CarRentalPro Call Support immediately.
5. If on a highway or dangerous location, call 112 (emergency services) first.

WRONG DRIVER ASSIGNED / DRIVER NOT ARRIVED:
1. Contact the driver using the mobile number in your booking confirmation email.
2. If unreachable, call CarRentalPro Call Support.
3. The admin will reassign or resolve the issue.

PAYMENT FAILED BUT MONEY DEDUCTED:
1. Go to Payment & Refund Support.
2. Provide your booking ID and bank transaction reference.
3. The team will verify and process a refund within 5-7 business days.
4. Razorpay usually reverses failed payment deductions automatically within 3-5 days.

PARCEL NOT DELIVERED:
1. Check the parcel status in Parcel > Track Parcel.
2. If status shows Accepted/Picked Up but not Delivered, contact Call Support.
3. Provide your parcel request ID.

COMMON FAQS:

Q: Can I change my pickup location after booking?
A: No, pickup location changes are not supported after booking. Cancel and rebook if needed.

Q: Can I extend my rental period?
A: Contact the car owner directly or call support to check if extension is possible before your drop time.

Q: What if I damage the rental car?
A: Any damages will be assessed. Costs are deducted from your security deposit. If damage exceeds the deposit, you will be billed for the difference.

Q: How do I get my security deposit back?
A: Deposits are refunded within 24 hours of car return, if the car is in good condition. Check your email for the refund notification.

Q: Can I book a car for someone else?
A: You can book in your account, but the primary driver must provide their license. You can add a nominee driver for Rental Only bookings.

Q: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
A: Go to Login > Forgot Password. Enter your registered email to receive a 6-digit OTP. Use the OTP to reset your password.
