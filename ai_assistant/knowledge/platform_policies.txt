Platform Policies for CarRentalPro (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

1. CANCELLATION POLICY:
- Users can cancel their bookings up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund.
- Cancellations within 24 hours of pickup will incur a 20% penalty fee.
- No-shows without cancellation will be charged the full amount.
- Luxury vehicle bookings require 48 hours advance cancellation for a full refund.
- To cancel a booking: Go to Customer > My Bookings > Select Booking > Cancel.

2. DRIVER / NOMINEE POLICY:
- In "With Driver" bookings, the driver is assigned by CarRentalPro and their details are shared via email before pickup.
- In "Rental Only", the person who booked the car must be the primary driver and provide driving license details.
- They can add a nominee driver (another person allowed to drive the car), but must be present at pickup.
- The nominee driver's license details must be provided at the time of booking.

3. CAR LISTINGS (OWNERS):
- Car owners can list their cars for rental or sale.
- All listings must be approved by the Admin before they appear on the platform.
- The Admin reviews verification documents like RC, insurance, and car photos.
- Rejected listings can be corrected and resubmitted.
- Owners can view listing status in Add Car > My Car Status.

4. PARCEL DELIVERY:
- Users can request a parcel delivery by filling out sender and receiver details.
- Drivers can accept or reject parcel requests.
- Once accepted, the sender receives a 12-digit QR code via email.
- To successfully pick up a parcel, the driver must verify the 12-digit code from the sender.
- To successfully deliver a parcel, the driver must ask the receiver for a 4-digit OTP.
- Prohibited items: illegal goods, flammable materials, weapons, live animals.

5. PAYMENT & SECURITY DEPOSIT:
- Security deposits must be paid during booking for "Rental Only" cars.
- Deposits are refunded within 24 hours of dropping off the car in good condition.
- Any damages incurred during rental will be deducted from the security deposit.
- Payments are processed via Razorpay (UPI, cards, net banking).

6. DAMAGE POLICY:
- Users are responsible for any damage to the rental car during their rental period.
- Damage costs are assessed and deducted from the security deposit.
- If damage cost exceeds the deposit, the user will be billed for the difference.
- Pre-existing damages must be noted during car inspection at pickup.

7. ACCOUNT POLICY:
- One email address can only be registered once.
- Users must be 18+ years of age to book a car.
- A valid Indian driving license is required for all Rental Only bookings.
- Accounts suspected of fraud will be suspended without notice.
- Password can be reset via Login > Forgot Password using an OTP sent to registered email.

8. INSURANCE POLICY:
- CarRentalPro offers optional rental insurance coverage.
- Basic coverage: Third-party liability (mandatory by law).
- Premium coverage: Comprehensive damage coverage including own damage.
- Insurance add-ons can be selected during the checkout process.
- In case of an accident, file an FIR, inform the car owner, and contact support immediately.

9. PRIVACY POLICY:
- User data (name, email, address, license) is stored securely in an encrypted database.
- CarRentalPro does not sell user data to third parties.
- Payment data is handled exclusively by Razorpay and is never stored on CarRentalPro servers.
- Users can request data deletion by contacting support.

10. PLATFORM ROLES:
- User/Renter: Books cars, sends parcels, buys cars.
- Owner: Lists cars for rental or sale.
- Driver: Accepts and completes trip and parcel assignments.
- Admin: Manages all platform operations, approvals, and assignments.
- Guest: Can browse listings but must log in to book, list, or send parcels.

11. SERVICEABLE AREAS:
- CarRentalPro is currently based in Hyderabad, Telangana, India.
- Doorstep delivery is available in Hyderabad and select surrounding areas.
- Outstation trips are supported; the route and distance determine the price.
- Interstate trips are allowed but must be declared at the time of booking.
