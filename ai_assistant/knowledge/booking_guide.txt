CarRentalPro — Car Booking Guide (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

HOW TO BOOK A CAR ON CARRENTAL PRO:

Step 1: Login to your account.
Step 2: Go to the Home page and click "Book a Car" or browse the car listings.
Step 3: You will be asked to choose your booking type:
  - "Rental Only" — You drive the car yourself. No driver provided.
  - "With Driver" — A professional driver is assigned by CarRentalPro and drives for you.

RENTAL ONLY BOOKING:
- Choose a car from available listings.
- Enter your pickup location, drop location, pickup date and time, drop date and time.
- Provide your driving license details.
- You can optionally add a nominee driver (another person allowed to drive the car).
- Pay the rental fee plus a security deposit during checkout.
- You will receive a booking confirmation email with all details.
- The security deposit is refunded within 24 hours after dropping off the car in good condition.
- Any damage to the car during the rental will be deducted from the security deposit.

WITH DRIVER BOOKING:
- Choose a car that supports "With Driver" mode.
- Enter your pickup and drop locations on the interactive map.
- The fare is calculated at approximately ₹15 per km. The exact price is generated on the booking map based on your actual route.
- Do NOT ask for a per-day price for With Driver bookings — the price depends on the route distance.
- After booking, a driver from CarRentalPro's approved driver pool will be assigned.
- You will receive the driver's name and mobile number via email before pickup.
- If no driver is immediately available, the booking goes to "Pending Platform Driver" status and an admin manually assigns one.

BOOKING STATUSES:
- Pending: Booking created, awaiting payment.
- Confirmed: Booking is confirmed and paid. Driver assigned (if applicable).
- Pending Platform Driver: Waiting for admin to assign a driver (for With Driver bookings).
- Ongoing: Trip is currently in progress.
- Completed: Trip is finished.
- Cancelled: Booking was cancelled by the user or admin.

KEY THINGS TO KNOW ABOUT BOOKING:
- Users must be logged in to book a car.
- Pickup and drop locations must be provided.
- Payment is handled securely via Razorpay.
- Users can view all their bookings under "My Bookings" in the customer section.
- Bookings can be cancelled up to 24 hours before pickup for a full refund.
- Cancellations within 24 hours incur a 20% fee.
- No-shows are charged the full amount.

HOW TO VIEW YOUR BOOKINGS:
- Go to the Customer section and click "My Bookings".
- You can see all your past and upcoming bookings with full details.
- Live tracking of your driver is available during an active trip on the "Live Track" page.

LIVE TRACKING:
- Once a trip starts, you can see the driver's live location on a map.
- Go to Customer > Live Track to use this feature.
- The map updates in real-time as the driver moves.
