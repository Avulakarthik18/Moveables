CarRentalPro — Payments & Checkout Guide (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

PAYMENT SYSTEM OVERVIEW:
CarRentalPro uses Razorpay as its secure payment gateway. All payments — for rentals, purchases, and (future) parcel deliveries — are processed through Razorpay.

PAYMENT METHODS ACCEPTED:
- UPI (GPay, PhonePe, Paytm, BHIM UPI)
- Debit cards and Credit cards
- Net banking
- Razorpay wallets

HOW CHECKOUT WORKS (BOOKING):

Step 1: After selecting a car and filling in booking details, click "Proceed to Payment."
Step 2: You are taken to the Checkout page, which shows:
  - Car name and details
  - Pickup and drop details
  - Rental type (Rental Only / With Driver)
  - Total rental cost
  - Security deposit (for Rental Only)
  - Grand total to be paid

Step 3: Click "Pay Now" to open the Razorpay payment popup.
Step 4: Choose your preferred payment method and complete the transaction.
Step 5: On successful payment:
  - Booking is confirmed and saved to the database.
  - You receive a booking confirmation email with all details.
  - You are redirected to the Payment Success page.
Step 6: On failed payment:
  - You are redirected to the Payment Failed page.
  - No booking is created.
  - If money was deducted, it will be automatically reversed by Razorpay within 3-5 days.
  - Contact Payment & Refund Support if not reversed within 7 days.

SECURITY DEPOSIT:
- Applicable only for "Rental Only" bookings.
- The deposit amount is set by the car owner when they list the car.
- It is collected at the time of booking (included in the grand total).
- Purpose: To cover any damages caused during the rental period.
- Refund: Within 24 hours of car return, if no damages are reported.
- If damage is found: The repair cost is deducted from the deposit, and the remainder is refunded.
- If zero damage: Full deposit is refunded.

REFUND POLICY:
- Full refund: Cancellation made more than 24 hours before pickup.
- Partial refund (80%): Cancellation within 24 hours of pickup (20% penalty).
- No refund: No-show (not arriving for pickup without cancellation).
- Security deposit refund: Within 24 hours of car return (if no damage).
- Payment failure refunds: Handled automatically by Razorpay within 3-5 business days.

FAILED PAYMENT / DOUBLE CHARGE:
- If payment failed but money was debited, the amount will be auto-reversed by Razorpay within 3-5 business days.
- If not reversed within 7 days, go to Services > Customer Support > Payment & Refund Support.
- Provide your transaction reference number and booking ID (if any) for investigation.

CHECKOUT PROCESS FOR CAR PURCHASE (BUY):
- When a seller accepts your bid, you are notified.
- Go to Sell/Buy > My Buy Requests to find the accepted bid.
- Click "Pay Now" to proceed to Razorpay checkout.
- Payment amount = your offered (bid) price.
- After payment, the purchase is marked as complete.
- Vehicle transfer (RC transfer, etc.) is handled directly between buyer and seller outside the platform.

INVOICES AND RECEIPTS:
- A booking confirmation email serves as your receipt.
- Razorpay also sends a payment receipt to your email directly.
- For GST invoices or formal receipts, contact CarRentalPro support.

IMPORTANT:
- Never share your OTP, UPI PIN, or bank password with anyone, including CarRentalPro staff.
- CarRentalPro will NEVER ask for your payment credentials directly.
- All payments are encrypted and processed securely through Razorpay.
