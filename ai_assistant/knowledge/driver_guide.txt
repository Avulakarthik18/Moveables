CarRentalPro — Driver Guide (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

OVERVIEW:
CarRentalPro has a registered driver network. Drivers are independent professionals who register on the platform and are assigned trips (with driver bookings) or can accept parcel delivery jobs.

HOW TO REGISTER AS A DRIVER:

Step 1: Go to the Driver Registration section on CarRentalPro.
Step 2: Fill in all required details:
  - Full name, date of birth, gender, address
  - Mobile number (must be unique — used as driver ID)
  - Email address
  - Driving license number and type (LMV, HMV, etc.)
  - License expiry date
  - Vehicle type you are registered for (Car, SUV, etc.)
  - Vehicle model you drive
  - Bank account details: Account number, IFSC code, UPI ID (for payment)
Step 3: Submit the registration form.
Step 4: CarRentalPro Admin reviews your application.
Step 5: Once approved, you appear in the driver pool and can be assigned trips.

DRIVER APPROVAL PROCESS:
- Registration status starts as "Pending."
- Admin reviews the license details and other credentials.
- If approved, status becomes "Approved" — you are now active on the platform.
- If rejected, you may reapply with corrected details.

GETTING ASSIGNED TO A TRIP:
- When a customer books a "With Driver" car, the admin sees the booking and checks available drivers.
- An admin assigns you to a trip if your schedule is free for those dates.
- You will receive a notification/email with the booking details: customer name, mobile, pickup and drop locations, dates and times.

HOW TO MANAGE A TRIP (TRIP CONTROL):
- Go to Driver > Trip Control to see your active and upcoming assignments.
- Before the trip starts: Review pick-up details and contact the customer if needed.
- Start the trip: Click "Start Trip" when you have picked up the customer.
- During the trip: Your live location is shared with the customer on their Live Track page.
- End the trip: Click "End Trip" when you reach the drop location.
- After the trip: The booking status updates to Completed automatically.

PARCEL DELIVERY (FOR DRIVERS):
- Drivers can also accept parcel delivery requests.
- Go to Driver > Trip Control or the parcel section to view pending parcel requests.
- Click Accept to take a parcel job. The sender will receive a 12-digit pickup code.
- At pickup: Ask the sender for the 12-digit code. Enter it to confirm parcel collection.
- At delivery: Ask the receiver for 4-digit OTP. Enter it to mark delivery as complete.
- You will receive payment for successful deliveries as per the platform rate.

DRIVER RULES AND CONDUCT:
- Always arrive on time for pickups. Contact the customer if delayed.
- If you cannot complete an assignment, notify admin immediately.
- Never share your login or account with another person.
- Maintain a professional appearance and attitude at all times.
- Do not smoke or allow smoking in the rental car.
- Ensure the car is kept clean throughout the trip.
- Report any accidents or incidents to CarRentalPro immediately.

PAYMENT TO DRIVERS:
- Driver earnings are calculated per trip based on route distance.
- Payments are settled to the driver's registered bank account or UPI ID.
- Parcel jobs have a separate fixed payout per successful delivery.
- Payment cycles and amounts are managed by the CarRentalPro admin team.

IMPORTANT:
- Drivers with expired licenses will be automatically removed from the active pool.
- Any misconduct reports from customers may result in suspension or removal.
- GPS location sharing is required during active trips for live tracking.
