CarRentalPro — Luxury Vehicles & Doorstep Delivery Guide (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

LUXURY VEHICLES SERVICE:

OVERVIEW:
CarRentalPro offers a premium Luxury Vehicles service for customers who want a high-end driving experience. This includes luxury sedans and luxury SUVs with professional drivers.

LUXURY SEDANS:
- Available luxury sedans include premium models from brands like Mercedes-Benz, BMW, Audi, Toyota Camry, Skoda Superb, and equivalent upper-segment cars.
- Luxury sedan bookings always include a professional driver — these are not self-drive.
- Ideal for: Corporate travel, airport transfers, business meetings, weddings, special occasions.
- How to book: Go to Services > Luxury Vehicles > Luxury Sedans.
- Select your preferred sedan, enter pickup and drop location, and book.
- Pricing is calculated per km (approximately ₹15-25/km depending on vehicle model).

LUXURY SUVs:
- Available luxury SUVs include premium models like Toyota Fortuner, Mahindra XUV700 (top trim), Hyundai Tucson, Kia Sorento, and equivalent.
- Luxury SUV bookings also always include a professional driver — these are not self-drive.
- Ideal for: Group travel, family outstation trips, offroad travel, VIP transfers.
- How to book: Go to Services > Luxury Vehicles > Luxury SUVs.
- Select your preferred SUV, enter pickup and drop location, and book.

LUXURY VEHICLE GENERAL POLICIES:
- All luxury bookings include a professional driver. No self-drive option.
- Advance booking is recommended (at least 24-48 hours before pickup).
- Cancellations for luxury bookings must be made 48 hours before pickup for a full refund.
- Damage to luxury vehicles will incur a higher damage fee compared to standard cars.
- Smoking is strictly not permitted in luxury vehicles.
- Drinking alcohol inside the vehicle is not permitted.
- Passengers are expected to maintain respectful conduct with the driver.


DOORSTEP DELIVERY SERVICE:

OVERVIEW:
CarRentalPro's Doorstep Delivery service brings the rental car directly to your doorstep. Instead of going to a pickup point, the driver delivers the car to your home or office.

HOW DOORSTEP DELIVERY WORKS:
Step 1: Go to Services > Doorstep Delivery.
Step 2: Or on the booking page, select "Doorstep Delivery" option.
Step 3: Enter your delivery address (home, office, or any accessible location).
Step 4: Choose delivery date and time window.
Step 5: The driver will bring the car to your location.
Step 6: Inspection is done on-site before you take the car.
Step 7: At the end of the rental, the driver picks the car up from your drop location.

CHOOSE DELIVERY LOCATION:
- You can see a map to select your precise delivery location.
- The system calculates if your location is within the serviceable delivery zone.
- Doorstep delivery is available in Hyderabad and select surrounding areas.
- Extra delivery charges may apply for locations beyond the standard zone (check on booking page).

DOORSTEP DELIVERY POLICIES:
- You must be present at the delivery location to receive the car.
- A valid driving license is required for Rental Only doorstep deliveries.
- The car will be inspected before handover. Note any pre-existing damage.
- Return pickup must be arranged in advance — inform the driver/admin of the final drop location.
- Doorstep delivery is not available for With Driver luxury bookings (driver manages logistics).

WHEN TO USE DOORSTEP DELIVERY:
- When you don't want to travel to a pickup point.
- For convenience during corporate or VIP bookings.
- When you need the car first thing in the morning.
- Available for both standard and premium car categories.
