CarRentalPro — Parcel Delivery Guide (RoadMind AI Knowledge Base)

OVERVIEW:
CarRentalPro offers a parcel delivery service where users can send parcels using CarRentalPro's network of approved drivers. Drivers pick up the parcel and deliver it to the receiver.

HOW TO SEND A PARCEL:

Step 1: Go to the Parcel section and click "Send Parcel".
Step 2: Fill in the parcel request form:
  - Pickup location (where the driver will collect the parcel from you)
  - Drop location (where the parcel needs to be delivered)
  - Parcel description (what is inside the parcel)
  - Parcel weight (approximate weight in kg)
  - Receiver's name
  - Receiver's mobile number
Step 3: Submit the parcel request.
Step 4: Drivers on the platform will see your request and can Accept or Reject it.
Step 5: Once a driver accepts, you will receive:
  - A confirmation email
  - A 12-digit verification QR code/code sent to your email
Step 6: When the driver arrives at your location for pickup:
  - Show the 12-digit pickup code or QR code to the driver.
  - The driver scans/enters this to confirm they picked up the correct parcel.
Step 7: The driver delivers the parcel to the receiver.
Step 8: At the time of delivery, the receiver must provide a 4-digit OTP to the driver.
  - This OTP is sent to the receiver's mobile number.
  - The driver enters this OTP in their app to mark the delivery as complete.
Step 9: You receive a delivery confirmation email once the parcel is delivered.

PARCEL DELIVERY STATUS:
- Pending: Parcel request submitted, waiting for a driver to accept.
- Accepted: A driver has accepted your parcel request.
- Picked Up: Driver has collected the parcel from your location (QR verified).
- Delivered: Parcel successfully delivered to the receiver (OTP verified).
- Rejected: No driver accepted within the window, or all drivers rejected.

HOW TO TRACK YOUR PARCEL:
- Go to the Parcel section and click "Track Parcel".
- Enter your parcel details to see the current status.
- You can see whether the parcel is Pending, Accepted, Picked Up, or Delivered.

IMPORTANT THINGS TO KNOW:
- The parcel service works through available CarRentalPro drivers who choose to accept requests.
- If no driver accepts, the parcel status remains Pending.
- You cannot cancel a parcel once a driver has accepted and picked it up.
- CarRentalPro is not responsible for damaged goods. Pack fragile items carefully.
- The 4-digit OTP protects against wrong-person delivery. The receiver MUST provide the OTP.
- Without the OTP, the driver cannot mark the delivery as complete.
- Parcel bookings are linked to driver trips — a driver may combine a rental with a parcel pickup if their route matches.

WHAT ITEMS ARE ALLOWED:
- Documents, files, and packages
- Electronics (packed safely)
- Clothing and personal items
- Small household goods
- Food items (perishable items must be clearly marked)

WHAT IS NOT ALLOWED:
- Illegal items, narcotics, weapons
- Flammable or hazardous materials
- Live animals
- Extremely heavy or oversized items (contact support for special arrangements)

SUPPORT FOR PARCEL ISSUES:
- If there is a problem with your parcel delivery, contact CarRentalPro support via the Customer Support section.
- Provide your parcel request ID and details for faster resolution.
